## Brand Description

Tesmaraneh is a sustainable, ethical fashion brand based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. It produces timeless wearable designs and accessories for women using environmentally friendly production practices. The brand specialises in West African local fabrics — Gara tie-dye, batiks, and woven/country cloth — and promotes these unique textiles to a global audience.

Tesmaraneh is also committed to reducing the importation of textiles and clothing into Sierra Leone, building a stronger local fashion and textile industry.
